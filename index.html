Pirates of the Pancreas

Haunted Liver

A haunted house built inside of Ruben's liver, which is damaged by strong alcoholism. It contains animatronic creatures, like a werewolf. Perfect place to get lost and never found (just kidding:).

It's A Small Small Intestine

It's a water based ride, located in Ruben's small intestine and features audio-animatronic children working a factory to simulate the workings of a small intestine. Easy ride to get to the colon.
